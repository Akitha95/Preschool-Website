Welcome To St. Georges Preschool

let us Introduce Ourselves

St George's Preschool is a small community based preschool in Eastwood. In 2013 AND AGAIN IN 2018 we received a rating of Exceeding the National Quality Standard in all Seven Quality Areas, a great result! We accept children onto our waiting list from 2 years of age and they are able to attend the preschool from 3 years of age (subject to a vacancy being available). We have a beautiful indoor environment with calming tones and abundant natural light. We also have a gorgeous outdoor environment full of exciting natural spaces to explore.
